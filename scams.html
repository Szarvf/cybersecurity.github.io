What are Scams?

Scams are deceptive schemes implemented to confuse users into giving away money, personal details, or access to accounts. Unlike more technical approaches scams are more reliant on social engineering by manipulating trust, emotions, or urgency. It can happen over the phone, through an email, or even from fake ads. Common types of scams include lottery winning messages, tech support scams, romance scams, and charity scams. They trick victims by exploiting their emotions and create a sense of authority over users using pressure tactics like "limited time offer" or "act now or miss out forever!" but can easily be prevented by being skeptical of things that are too good to be true.
