Network Security



One of the most major cybersecurity solutions and one that many individuals, businesses, and even governments, rely on. It involves looking after communication networks such as devices, hardware, software, and many other communication protocols. It's main goal is to increase confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information for all users such as Wi-Fi and company networks.



Key Components

  • Firewalls - Barrier between trusted and untrusted networks
  • Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDS/IPS)
  • Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) for secure and remote access
  • Encryption for data in transit


Common Network Threats

  • Hackers exploiting vulnerabilities
  • Malware spread across multiple networks
  • Man-In-The-Middle attacks (hacker altering communication between two parties)
  • Phishing that can compromise network access
