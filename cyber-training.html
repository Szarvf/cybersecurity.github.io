Cyber-Security Knowledge/Training Levels

Level 1: Young Children Knowledge

Aware of basic coding and computer skills from technology classes, devlop curiosity in technology and how the internet works.

Level 2: High School Knowledge

Focus on digital technology classes and learn fundamentals of cybersecurity, safe browsing, and password management.

Level 3: Introduction to Cybersecurity Concepts

Learn about common cyber threats including malware, phishing, and ransomware. Understand importance of online safety and ethical uses of technology.

Level 4: Hands-on Practice and Small Projects

Experiment in safe virtual environments (sandboxing, coding exercises). Practie creating strong passwords, encrypting files, and using anti-virus software.

Level 5: Advanced Cyber Skills and Tools (About where I am at)

Learn about endpoint security, firewalls, and network protection. Explore tools like virtual machines, VPNs, and penetration testing basics.

Level 6: Competitions and Real World Challenges

Participate in cybersecurity competitions or online challenges (CTFs), apply skills to solve practical problems in a safe setting.

Level 7: Continuous Learning and Certification

Take online courses, attend workshops, or earn certificates while staying updated with emerging threats, best security practices, and industry trends to possibly begin a career in the field.
